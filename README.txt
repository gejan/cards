Minetest Cards Mod
--------------------------

Adds cards to Minetest.


Depends on nothing.

Crafting recipes require Minetest Game's dye and default mods:
Recipe of cardbox:
default:chest, 	   dye:black,  	  dye:green,
default:clay_lump, dye:red,   	  dye:blue,
default:paper,     default:paper, default:paper
